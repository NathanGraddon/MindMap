# MindMap
MindMap is a guided decision-support platform for college students and early-career users that helps them work through difficult personal, academic, social, and career-related decisions in a healthier and more responsible way.

Instead of making choices for the user, it:

  -helps them break down a difficult situation

  -evaluates the decision from multiple perspectives

  -shows likely consequences

  -highlights ethical and emotional considerations

  -guides them toward a choice that supports well-being, responsibility, and long-term success

That makes it much deeper than a simple advice app.
